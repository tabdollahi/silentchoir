REPORTS CAN TRIGGER AN EMOTIONAL RESPONSE

In the following, we will ask you a series of questions related to your experience. Although we aim to ask as little as needed, there are certain questions and details we must request for your legal protection.

These questions may trigger you to relive trauma related to your experiences. Please call the National Sexual Assault Telephone Hotline (800.656.HOPE (4673)) if you need to speak with a trained professional.

Note: If anything in this questionnaire makes you feel uncomfortable or induces any unwanted stress, please do not hesitate to use the National Sexual Assault Telephone Hotline (800.656.HOPE (4673)) to be connected with a trained staff member from a sexual assault service provider in your area. This hotline is operated by RAINN who can provide counseling and advisory services for survivors.
