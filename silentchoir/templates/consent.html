ARE YOU COMFORTABLE REPORTING

Disclosing violence and other abuses can be an intimate, delicate, and often a painful process that can trigger a number of emotional reactions. While your participation can be a healing and meaningful contribution to combating rape culture, you are in no way obligated to share your story.

Please consider whether this is the right time for you to be completing this report. Do you have a support system or other resources to access should you feel triggered? Are you in a safe place? Please call the National Sexual Assault Telephone Hotline (800.656.HOPE (4673)) if you need to speak with a trained professional.

Note: If anything in this questionnaire makes you feel uncomfortable or induces any unwanted stress, please do not hesitate to use the National Sexual Assault Telephone Hotline (800.656.HOPE (4673)) to be connected with a trained staff member from a sexual assault service provider in your area. This hotline is operated by RAINN who can provide counseling and advisory services for survivors.
